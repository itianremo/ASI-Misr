About ASI-CRM

ASI Customer Relationship Management (ASI-CRM) is a Web-based application designed and developed to be ASI's reliable tool for the management of all customer relationships, as well as the facilitation of the processes of adding, editing and/or retrieving customer account and contact information.

 

The ASI-CRM Web based application offers proven benefits and contains powerful features. 

ASI-CRM Benefits

The ASI- CRM application offers the following benefits for ASI:

  • Cost-savings through improvement in product delivery and response time to customer requests.
  • Improved efficiencies in the value chain through linkage of ASI customers to suppliers.
  • Improved focus on high value customers.
  • Improved understanding of the individual customer purchase life-cycle.
  • Improved understanding of customer profitability.
  • Channeling certain sales to call centers and/or Web sites. 
  • Reduction in customer churn.
  • More responsive technical support.

Key Product Features

The ASI- CRM application offers the following features:

  • Basic and advanced search for customer accounts and contacts.
  • Ability to sort data by almost all grid headers.
  • Ability to print different reports that match your reporting requirements (to be available in later versions).
  • Powerful and high-level security.
  • Availability for usage at all times on a 24/7 basis.
  • Reliability; it enables multiple users to access the application concurrently.
  • Ease of use; it enables users - according to their security settings - to add, edit, search, and view customer and contact data in an easy way.
