Searching for an Existing Customer Account

This procedure enables you to search for an existing customer account for the purpose of

  • editing/updating their account information,
  • editing/updating their customer contacts,
  • adding/updating their notes, or
  • only viewing their account information saved in the ASI customer database.

Prerequisites 

  • You are a member of the division(s); e.g., Customer Relations, that can access the ASI-CRM application.

Notes

  • Contact your network administrator for detailed information on your ASI CRM level of security settings
  • You may use the Accounts page as your search tool for existing customer accounts in case you want to tag customer accounts.
To search for an existing customer account, perform the following steps:
  1. Log into the ASI-CRM application.
  2. On the main menu bar, click . The Account Details page is opened. Click to see figure.

  3. Type in the first letter(s) of the customer's name in the Find Company text box. The application displays a list of names beginning with the letter(s) you have just typed in.
  4. Highlight the target customer's name and click your mouse or press Enter. Click to see figure.

  5. Click the Search button. The customer's account details are displayed for you.
  6. Click on the toolbar, if you want to edit/update the current customer account information.
  7. Click the Contacts tab on the bottom part of the page, if you want to edit/update contacts for the current customer account.
  8. Click the Notes tab on the bottom part of the page, if you want to add/update notes on the current customer account.
