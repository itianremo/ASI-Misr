Tagging customers or contacts

This procedure enables you to place a tag, i.e., a flag, on one or more customer account or contact records. Tagging is a useful way for marking special customers and/or contacts, based on good business reasons.

Prerequisites  

  • You are a member of the division(s); e.g., Customer Relations, that can access the ASI-CRM application.
  • Your CRM security settings allow you to tag/un-tag customer and/or contact records.

Note: Contact your network administrator for detailed information on your ASI CRM level of security settings.
To tag customer or contact record(s), perform the following steps:
  1. Log into ASI-CRM application.
  2. Under the Tag column header, go to the record you want to flag and click inside the box available for it.
  3. Repeat the above step if you want to place a flag on individual data records.
  4. If you want to remove a flag form a tagged record, go to that record and deselect the box available for it.
  5. If you want to flag all records displayed in one page, click the button.
  6. If you want to remove the flag from all records displayed in one page, click the button.
  7. Click Save. The application processes this operation and saves your change on that record. Later, you can sort the data records according to their tagging or un-tagging status.
