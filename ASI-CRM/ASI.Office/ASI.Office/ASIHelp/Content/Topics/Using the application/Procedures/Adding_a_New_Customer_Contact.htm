Adding a New Customer Contact

This procedure enables you to add a new customer contact into the ASI customer database. Once the new customer contact record is added to the database, it cannot be deleted except by the authorized database administrator(s).

Prerequisites 

  • You are a member of the division(s); e.g., Customer Relations, that can access the ASI-CRM application.
  • Your CRM security settings allow you to add a new customer contact.

Note: Contact your network administrator for detailed information on your ASI CRM level of security settings.
To add a new customer contact, perform the following steps:
  1. Log into the ASI-CRM application.
  2. On the main menu bar, click . The Contact Details page is opened. Click to see figure.

  3. On the toolbar, click . A new contact record options open for you to configure the new customer contact information. The Company/Agency is a view-only section and cannot be edited in this page. Go to the Contact section.
  4. In the Company/Agency section, select the target company name from the available drop-down list. The company contact details are displayed, if available. Go to the Contact section.

  5. Type in the new contact name. You can enter the full first and last names and a title; e.g., Mr., Ms., or Jr., before or after the name. If you enter the last name first, use a comma (,) or a semicolon (;) to separate it from the first name. For example, you can enter a name in the following formats:

    - Mr. John Williams

    - Williams, Chris

    - Williams; Chris

  6. Select the title applicable to the new contact from the choices available in the Title drop-down list; e.g., Sr. Sales Manager. If you are unsure about the title, leave it blank or select Other.
  7. Select the business department of the new contact from the choices available in the Department drop-down list; e.g., Engineering.
  8. Configure the contact information of the new contact in the available contact controls/fields; i.e., Phone, Ext., Cell Phone, Fax, E-mail. Go to the Miscellaneous section.
  9. Select an identification for the new customer contact from the choices available in the ID/Status drop-down list; e.g. Competitor.
  10. Enter the birthday date of the new contact in the Birthday field. You may type in the date or use the available calendar instead.
  11. Enter the spouse name of the new contact, if available, in the Spouse field.
  12. Enter the name of the person/entity that referred the new contact to ASI, if available, in the Referred By field.
  13. Type in special notes on the new contact, if any, in the Notes editing box.
  14. Click the Notes tab on the bottom part of the page, if you want to add new notes on the newly added customer contact.
  15. Click to save the new contact data to the ASI customer database; otherwise, click .
