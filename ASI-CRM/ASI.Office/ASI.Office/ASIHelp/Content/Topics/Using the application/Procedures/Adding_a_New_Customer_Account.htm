Adding a New Customer Account

This procedure enables you to add a new customer account into the ASI customer database. Once the new customer account record is added to the database, it cannot be deleted except by the authorized database administrator(s).

Prerequisites 

  • You are a member of the division(s); e.g., Customer Relations, that can access the ASI-CRM application.
  • Your CRM security settings allow you to add a new customer account.

Note: Contact your network administrator for detailed information on your ASI CRM level of security settings.
To add a new customer account, perform the following steps:
  1. Log into the ASI-CRM application.
  2. On the main menu bar, click . The Account Details page is opened. Click to see figure.

  3. On the toolbar, click . A new record opens for you to configure the new customer account.
  4. Enter the name of the new customer in the Company/Agency text box.
  5. Select the business sector for the new customer from the choices available in the drop-down list; e.g. PR Company.
  6. Enter the new customer telephone number in the Telephone text box.
  7. Enter the new customer fax number in the Fax text box.
  8. Enter the URL of the new customer in the Web site text box.
  9. Configure the new customer address in the available address fields; i.e., Street 1, Street 2, City, Zip Code, and Country.
  10. Enter the business category or identification of the new customer by selecting one of the available choices in the ID/Status drop-down list; e.g. Contractor.
  11. Enter the name of the person/entity that referred the new customer, if any, in the Referred by text box.
  12. Select the ASI account manager for the new customer from the Account Manager drop-down list.
  13. If you want to add new contacts for the newly added customer account, click the Contacts tab on the bottom part of the page.
  14. If you want to add new notes on the newly added customer account, click the Notes tab on the bottom part of the page.
  15. If you want to save the new data to the ASI customer database, click .
  16. If you want to cancel the data you have added, click .
