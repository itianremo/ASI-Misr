Editing an Existing Customer Contact

This procedure enables you to edit/update an existing customer contact. Editing/updating a customer contact may be in the form of changing address information due to a contact relocation, or changing their identification in the ASI customer database. Further, you can add new or update existing notes on that contact.

Prerequisites 
  • You are a member of the division(s); e.g., Customer Relations, that can access the ASI-CRM application.
  • Your CRM security settings allow you to update an existing customer contact.

Note: Contact your network administrator for detailed information on your ASI CRM level of security settings.
