Editing an Existing Customer Account

This procedure enables you to edit/update an existing customer account. Editing/updating a customer's account may be in the form of changing address information due to a customer relocation, changing their identification in the ASI customer database, or adding/updating contacts for that customer.

Prerequisites 

  • You are a member of the division(s); e.g., Customer Relations, that can access the ASI-CRM application.
  • Your CRM security settings allow you to update an existing customer account.

Note: Contact your network administrator for detailed information on your ASI CRM level of security settings.
To edit/update an existing customer account, perform the following steps:
  1. Log into the ASI-CRM application.
  2. On the main menu bar, click . The Account Details page is opened. Click to see figure.

  3. On the toolbar, click . All controls/fields on the page become active.
  4. Enter your edits/updates in the controls/fields you want.
  5. Click the Contacts tab on the bottom part of the page, if you want to edit/update contacts for the current customer account.
  6. Click the Notes tab on the bottom part of the page, if you want to edit/update notes on the current customer account.
  7. If you want to save your edits, click . The application displays a confirmation message that the account has been updated successfully.
  8. If you want to cancel all changes, click .
