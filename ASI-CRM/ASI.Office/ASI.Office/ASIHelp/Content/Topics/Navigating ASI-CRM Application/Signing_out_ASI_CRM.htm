Signing out from the ASI-CRM Application

This procedure enables you to log out from the ASI-CRM application.

To log out from the ASI-CRM application, perform the following steps:
  1. In the upper right hand corner of any active application page, click Sign Out.
  2. The application closes the active page and returns you to the ASI CRM Login page.
